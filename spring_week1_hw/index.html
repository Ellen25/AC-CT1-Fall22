Data Collection

The data I will collect would be how many water I drink everyday, like a way to stimulate myself to drink more water. I will use a water level sensor to detect how many water would be left during a period of time
the data collection method (physically): everytime I drink some water, I will pour some water out of the glass to maintain the same water level with the cup I used to drink.

How often will you measure and how often will you record the values?

The sensor will be put inside a cup for one afternoon (or a period of time as long as much data has been collected

Will you record all the values or only the changes?

All the values will be collected and will be presented in a sequence so that a pattern can be found

When will the translation of raw values into meaningful data take place - before or after the recording?

After the recording. So that a reflection and a retrospect on the performance of my drinking habit can be summarized
